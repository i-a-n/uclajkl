Senior Exhibition: Are You Ready to Rock

Thursday, June 7 - Sat, June 16

Reception: Thurs, June 7, 5:00-8:00pm

Are You Ready to Rock is an end-of-year exhibition that features UCLA Department of Art undergraduate senior students. Please join us on Thursday, June 7 from 5-8pm for the opening reception. The gallery will also be open before and after Commencement on June 16 so that visiting family and friends may attend.

New Wight Gallery

1100 Broad Art Center

Los Angeles, CA 90095

Gallery Hours:

Monday-Friday, 9:00AM-4:30PM

*Except Friday, June 15: 9am-2pm & Saturday, June 16: 2-4pm and 6-7pm

(to accommodate Commencement activities)

Admission is free.

Parking in Lot 3 is $3/hour, max $12/day.

Directions to Broad Art Center

For more information, call (310) 825-0557.
